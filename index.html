About The Game

Medieval Maze is a medieval themed pseudo 3D computer game. It has medieval walls you can't walk through and it has medieval doors you can open with keys. There will also be medieval guards that are chasing you to kill you because you entered their world uninvited. Your task now is, to use keys you get/ have to find the end of the maze(finish door) without getting caught by guards.

For this MVP there will only be two rounds with increased number of guards and complex map. You can use 'A' or '⬅' keys to look to your left. 'D' or '➡' keys to look to your right. 'W' or '⬆' keys to move forward. 'S' or ⬇' keys to move backwards. If you have enough amount of keys for the doors you can open any door pressing the 'E' key.
